Beef Ragu Recipe

Slow Cooked Beef Ragu with Pappardelle

A cheap piece of beef, a few household ingredients, cooked low and slow. This Slow Cooked Beef Ragu is hearty, tasty, and one of my personal favourite recipes.

Ingredients

  • 1.2kg chuck beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of carrots, diced
  • 1 cup of celery, diced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 800g crushed tomatoes
  • 3 tbsp of tomato paste
  • 2 beef stock cubes
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1.5 cups of water
  • 3 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • 3 dried bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Season beef with salt and pepper
  2. Add 1 tbsp of olive oil to a pot and sear beef over high heat until browned. Remove and place on a plate.
  3. Turn stove down to medium low, add remaining olive oil, then saute onion and garlic for 2 minutes. Next add carrots and celery and saute for 5 minutes
  4. Add remaining ingredients and return beef to pot (including juices). Bring to a simmmer, then turn down to a slow bubble.
  5. Cover the pot with lid and slow cook for 2 hours or until beef can be shredded.
  6. Shred beef with 2 forks, add back to pot, simmer for 30 more minutes (or until sauce has thickened).
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste, then serve.
