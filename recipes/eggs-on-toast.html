Eggs on Toast Recipe

Fried Eggs on Toast

A couple of fresh eggs, salt and pepper, some buttered toast. This Fried Eggs on Toast Recipe is loved by many and will stand the test of time. What's better?. It's quick, easy, and nutritious!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 pieces of bread
  • 1 tbsp of your preffered oil
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Add 1 tbsp of oil to a pan and bring to medium heat
  2. Chuck your bread into the toaster and whack that bad boy on.
  3. Crack the two eggs into the pan
  4. Cover the pan with it's lid so that the top of the egg cooks
  5. Once the toast has popped up, butter it to your liking.
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste, then chow down!
