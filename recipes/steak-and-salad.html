Seared Steak with Green Salad

A delicious piece of scotch-fillet or porterhouse, a few greens, and some simple seasonings. This Seared Steak with Green Salad is healthy, tasty, and ready in no time!

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of Scotch Fillet or Porterhouse Steak
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 bag leafy greens
  • 1/2 cucumber, chopped
  • 1/4 red onion, coarsely diced
  • 1 avocado (optional)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Prepare cucumber, red onion and avocado
  2. Season beef with salt, pepper, and garlic powder
  3. Add 1 tbsp of olive oil to a pan and sear beef over high heat (approx. 2 mins per side for medium-rare)
  4. Whilst steaks are cooking, add leafy greens into a salad bowl, then add the cucumber, red onion, and avocado.
  5. Add balsamic vinegar, remaining olive oi, salt, and pepper to the salad, then mix.
  6. Serve salad, then place steak either on top or to the side.
  7. Slam that tasty goodness down!
